## **Marketing Funnel \& Conversion Performance Dashboard**



#### **Project Overview**

This project presents an interactive \*\*Marketing Funnel Dashboard\*\* built using \*\*Power BI\*\* to analyze customer journey and conversion performance.



The dashboard helps in understanding how users move through different stages of the funnel:

\*\*Visitors → Leads → Customers\*\*



##### &#x20;**Objectives**

\- Analyze marketing funnel performance  

\- Identify drop-off points in the conversion process  

\- Evaluate channel and campaign effectiveness  

\- Compare device-based user behavior  

\- Support data-driven marketing decisions  



##### &#x20;**Key Features**

\- 📊 Funnel Visualization (Visitor → Lead → Customer)

\- 📉 Drop-off Analysis across stages  

\- 📈 Customer \& Revenue Trends over time  

\- 🌳 Channel \& Campaign Performance (Treemap)  

\- 🍩 Device Distribution Analysis  

\- 🎛️ Interactive Filters (Channel, Campaign, Device)  



###### &#x20;**Key Insights**

\- High drop-off observed at \*\*Visitor → Lead stage\*\*  

\- \*\*Google channel\*\* shows better conversion performance  

\- \*\*Campaign B\*\* generates more customers  

\- \*\*Mobile users\*\* have lower conversion rates compared to desktop  



**Recommendations**

\- Improve landing page experience to reduce drop-offs  

\- Focus on high-performing channels like Google  

\- Optimize underperforming campaigns  

\- Enhance mobile user experience  

\- Retarget users who drop off early in the funnel
